If you've managed to career switch into tech from a different field,
congratulations! It can be a wonderful field to be in, and you should be proud;
that's no easy feat.

One of the next steps you can take is sharing your journey. Here are a few key
things to keep in mind when crafting your story.

### 

- Deciding on the best medium
- Mention how easy it was, and tell other people how to do it
- Bury the lede on things that made your journey easier
- Don't mention various privileges that made it easier
